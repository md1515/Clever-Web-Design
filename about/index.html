What to expect

When you work with Clever much of the process is fairly predictable. Here's a quick run down of how the process works for our web design and app development services:

  • 1      First Contact

    When you first call we'll want you to explain in your own words what you have in mind. If you're not quite sure what you're looking for that's okay too. We'll ask you a few more questions while we take notes and, if we can, give you a ballpark estimate of what the cost would be and our general approach to your project. We'll then ask to schedule a meeting with you in the next 5 to 7 days.
     
  • 2      Research and Proposal

    During that time we'll be putting together a formal proposal based on our previous conversation with you. Our proposal outlines a minimum of three different approaches to the same project which you get to then choose from according to which one best suits your goals and budget.
     
  • 3      Terms and Agreement

    Once you've chosen which solution makes sense to you, we'll put in writing exactly what we promised and then begin the work. Before we begin, however, we will ask for a portion of the cost as a deposit.
     
  • 4      Our Work is in Your Hands

    We'll be in touch regularly during the course of the work. We'll get your opinions and input while we bring the project together. When the work is finished we'll hand over the completed project, set up live online, and even show you how to use your new website and any tools we've set up for you in addition to the instruction manuals we provide.
Skills and abilities

We're experts in web media. That means both content creation and marketing. Our biggest strength is our modern techniques. The world of web technologies is fast paced and those who can't keep up get left behind. That's the reason you see so many larger web design companies failing these days. They learned what they know back in the late to mid 90's, got set in their ways, and now their quickly falling behind. Everyone here at Clever Web Design is not only trained in modern web standards and best practices but must learn a new skill every six months and apply that skill to a personal project. At the end of each year we all vote on who built the coolest web app and they get a prize (this year's prize is an iPad2).

Masters in the art of...

We specialize in the use of modern web standards. That's not just a buzz word we use to sell our wares, it's the truth. Everything we set out to work on is built with the future in mind. We ask ourselves questions like "Is this design trendy or will it still look good in 5 years?" and "How can I leverage new code specs to make this design actually become more impressive as time goes on?" or "How will the evolution of mobile browsers affect the layout and load times of this site in the future?". We're masters in HTML5, CSS3, PHP, MySQL, Ruby (and Rails), JavaScript, and more. On top of that we're constantly adding to the list of languages we develop in. That means you take home a product that is built to last and won't need to be redesigned in a few years time.
